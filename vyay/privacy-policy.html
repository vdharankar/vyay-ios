Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Vyay. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights.

Data We Collect

We collect and process the following types of data:

  • Expense information you enter (amount, description, category)
  • List names and categories you create
  • Device information (for app functionality)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Store your expense records and lists
  • Improve our app's features and user experience

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. We use Core Data for local storage, ensuring your information remains private and secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@vyay.app
